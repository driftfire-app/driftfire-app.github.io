Support

Thanks for playing Driftfire! Here's how to get help, report a problem, or suggest something.

Report a bug or request a feature

The fastest way is to open an issue on our public tracker — you can see what's already been reported and add your own:

Open an issue

A free GitHub account is required to file an issue.

Prefer email?

Email driftfire.app@gmail.com and we'll get back to you. If you're reporting a problem, please include your device model, your iOS/iPadOS version, and what you were doing when it happened — it helps a lot.

Frequently asked

Do I need an internet connection?
No — Driftfire is pass-and-play on a single device.

Does it collect my data?
No. See the Privacy Policy.
