Privacy Policy

Effective date: [TO BE SET AT PUBLISH]

Short version: Driftfire collects nothing. The game has no accounts, runs entirely on your device, and sends none of your information anywhere.

What we collect

Nothing. Driftfire does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal information. There are no user accounts and no sign-in.

Analytics, ads, and tracking

None. Driftfire contains no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking SDKs. It does not build a profile of you or track you across apps or websites.

Data stored on your device

Your settings and game progress are saved locally on your device so the game remembers your preferences. This information never leaves your device and is removed when you delete the app.

Network

Driftfire is a local, pass-and-play game. It does not require an internet connection to play and does not transmit personal data over the network.

Children's privacy

Because Driftfire collects no personal information from anyone, it collects none from children. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or the equivalent age in your region).

Third-party software

Driftfire is built with the open-source Godot Engine. The engine does not collect personal data on our behalf.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update it on this page and revise the effective date above.
